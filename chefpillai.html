Chef Pillai

Social Media PostsChef's Discovery

Chef Suresh Pillai, today’s special ambassador of Kerala cuisine to the world and Super Master Chef to over 1.7 million of his social media followers, has not lost that simplicity of an Island boy from the banks of the Ashtamudi lake in Kollam, Kerala. From his humble beginnings in Kerala, he spent 27 years honing his culinary skills in multiple kitchens including the BBC Masterchef Professionals. His travels took him to islands such as England, Australia and the Caribbean Islands as a culinary mentor to University students.

In spite of all his culinary experiences throughout the world, he retains the charming humility of an Island boy on a wooden canoe in the placidly beautiful Ashtamudi lake in Kerala. Today, Chef Suresh Pillai, the island boy sees the whole world as an island, and just as equally exciting. From fulfilling every Chef’s dream of opening his very own restaurant, Chef Suresh did this with astounding appreciation and feedback in Bengaluru, India’s garden city and tech capital.

He has since opening RESTAURANT CHEF PILLAI in Whitefield, Bengaluru, opened his new restaurant in the iconic Le Meridian Hotel in Kochi under the MARRIOT banner. He is now set to open his signature RESTAURANT CHEF PILLAI brand in Dubai, UAE and in Doha, Qatar. In addition to RCP restaurant brands, he has also launched his fast-food or rather ‘Food on the Go’ brand KOTHU XPRESS and due for launch is his specialised Tandoori cuisine brand NORTH RASOI.

The very essence of Chef Suresh Pillai’s magical Kerala flavours is his unique, signature way of blending traditional spices that impart ‘flavour bomb’ potential to all his cuisine. His guests, literally attain NIRVANA, after flavouring one of his signature dishes. His menu creations are challenging and adventurous, and gives the uniqueness of Kerala cuisine a flavourful twist. LIke he says "The two most important ingredients on our menu is love and simplicity".

The simplicity can be traced back to his humble island upbringing and love? He just lets love flow, both in way of cooking and his attitude to life. What drives him is his team, he says. Every one on my team is as important as a guest. One gives what one gets, naturally. That is why ‘ Let our food do the talking’, he says with a sparkle in his eyes.
